# 🎨 Brand Identity Guide

## Brand Overview

**Spice & Grain** is a premium Indian grocery ecommerce brand specializing in pulses, dry fruits, spices, and masala powders. The brand represents quality, authenticity, tradition, and trust.

## Brand Values

1. **Purity**: 100% natural products with no artificial additives
2. **Quality**: Premium sourcing and quality control
3. **Trust**: Transparent practices and honest communication
4. **Tradition**: Respecting Indian culinary heritage
5. **Innovation**: Modern approach to traditional products

## Logo Concept

### Primary Logo
```
🌾 Spice & Grain
   Pure & Natural
```

### Logo Elements
- **Icon**: Wheat/grain symbol (🌾) representing natural, farm-fresh products
- **Wordmark**: "Spice & Grain" in bold, modern font
- **Tagline**: "Pure & Natural" in lighter weight

### Logo Variations
1. **Full Logo**: Icon + Wordmark + Tagline
2. **Horizontal**: Icon + Wordmark (for headers)
3. **Icon Only**: For app icon and favicon
4. **Wordmark Only**: For minimal spaces

## Typography

### Font Families

**Primary Font: Inter**
- Usage: Body text, UI elements, descriptions
- Weights: 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium), 600 (Semibold)
- Characteristics: Clean, modern, highly readable

**Display Font: Poppins**
- Usage: Headings, titles, hero text
- Weights: 600 (Semibold), 700 (Bold), 800 (Extrabold)
- Characteristics: Friendly, geometric, impactful

### Type Scale

```
H1: 48px / 3rem (Poppins Bold)
H2: 36px / 2.25rem (Poppins Bold)
H3: 30px / 1.875rem (Poppins Semibold)
H4: 24px / 1.5rem (Poppins Semibold)
H5: 20px / 1.25rem (Poppins Semibold)
H6: 18px / 1.125rem (Poppins Semibold)

Body Large: 18px / 1.125rem (Inter Regular)
Body: 16px / 1rem (Inter Regular)
Body Small: 14px / 0.875rem (Inter Regular)
Caption: 12px / 0.75rem (Inter Regular)
```

## Visual Style

### Photography Style
- **Natural lighting**: Bright, warm, inviting
- **Close-ups**: Show product texture and quality
- **Lifestyle shots**: Products in use, cooking scenes
- **Color grading**: Warm tones, slightly saturated
- **Backgrounds**: Clean, minimal, white or wooden surfaces

### Illustration Style
- **Flat design**: Simple, modern illustrations
- **Line art**: Clean lines for icons and graphics
- **Color usage**: Brand colors with gradients
- **Style**: Friendly, approachable, not too corporate

### Iconography
- **Style**: Lucide React icons (consistent, modern)
- **Size**: 20px, 24px, 32px standard sizes
- **Stroke**: 2px stroke width
- **Color**: Match brand colors or neutral gray

## Brand Voice

### Tone
- **Warm**: Friendly and welcoming
- **Trustworthy**: Honest and reliable
- **Knowledgeable**: Expert but not condescending
- **Approachable**: Easy to understand
- **Enthusiastic**: Passionate about quality

### Writing Style
- Use simple, clear language
- Avoid jargon
- Be conversational but professional
- Use active voice
- Keep sentences short and punchy

### Example Copy

**Good:**
"Fresh, premium quality almonds delivered to your door. No preservatives, just pure goodness."

**Avoid:**
"We provide high-quality almond products through our advanced supply chain management system."

## Marketing Materials

### Product Photography Guidelines
1. Use natural lighting
2. Show product packaging clearly
3. Include lifestyle shots
4. Maintain consistent background
5. Show product scale with props

### Banner Design
- Hero text: Large, bold, Poppins
- Subtext: Medium size, Inter
- CTA button: Prominent, saffron
- Background: Product image with overlay
- Layout: Left-aligned text, right-aligned product

### Social Media
- **Instagram**: Square (1080x1080), lifestyle focus
- **Facebook**: Landscape (1200x630), promotional
- **Twitter**: Landscape (1200x675), quick tips
- **WhatsApp**: Square (800x800), offers

## Brand Don'ts

❌ Don't use the logo on busy backgrounds
❌ Don't stretch or distort the logo
❌ Don't use colors outside the palette
❌ Don't use multiple fonts
❌ Don't use low-quality images
❌ Don't be overly formal or corporate
❌ Don't make false claims
❌ Don't use clipart or generic stock photos

## Brand Checklist

✅ Logo visible and clear
✅ Brand colors used consistently
✅ Typography follows guidelines
✅ Images are high quality
✅ Tone of voice is appropriate
✅ Mobile-friendly design
✅ Accessible to all users
✅ Fast loading times
✅ Clear call-to-actions
✅ Consistent across all touchpoints

---

**Remember**: Every interaction with customers is an opportunity to reinforce the brand. Stay consistent, stay authentic, stay premium.
